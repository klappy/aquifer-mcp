## What This Service Does

Bible Aquifer is a read-only service that provides AI assistants with access to openly-licensed Bible scholarship resources from the BibleAquifer collection. It serves study notes, dictionaries, translation guides, maps, images, and related content through the Model Context Protocol (MCP).

The service does not require an account, does not accept user-generated content, and does not process payments.
